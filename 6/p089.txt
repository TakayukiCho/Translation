Summary:
Polymanga is the Western Switzerland's convention specialized in Japanese and Western pop-culture, manga, anime and video games.
It is held at the Montreux Conference Center at 2m2c in Easter every year since 2013.
Visitors participate in the event with their families, all the family enjoy the best entertainment at the 18,000m2 venue every year on Easter day.
There was a huge program in 2015: more than 50 guests, concerts, screenings, webseries, more than 12 video game tournaments, publisher showroom, 4 cosplay parades, shopping galleries, karaoke and etc.
