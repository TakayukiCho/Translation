Summary:
Higan was the first anime event in Belarus, the first festival was held in 2006 as meeting plañe for fans of Japanese anime.
Since then, Higan is held as event by fans for fans.
"Higan" is the day, when day and night are equal in Japan, and it named the event so that it is the meaning that tradition and modern culture are equally connected. (??)
The event aims to provide young people the opportunity to raise self-realization and creativity.
It has become an event focusing not only on Japan, but on all East Asian pop-culture as well in recent years.
Higan holds 2 days every year.
Cosplay and the theater performances, dance and karaoke on the stage, AMV screening and etc. are done for 13 to 15 hours including break time. 
There are also selling venue of doujinshi and cosplay photos, doll exhibitions and game corner in the event.
