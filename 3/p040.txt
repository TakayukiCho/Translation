Summary:
Anime Midwest is the 3-days anime event in Chikago, organized by fans for fans.
Anime Midwest performs a lot of small events such as cosplay, screenings of anime, panel lecture, video games and free distibution of drinks.
This event is the meeting place for otaku, especially those who loves Japanese anime.
We (AnimeCon.org) are planning for fans several anime events in a year. 
A lot of anime events are held in Chicago, but Anime Midwest is considered to be one of the great events.
