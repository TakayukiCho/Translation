Summary:
Animethon is an anime event that started in 1994 to spread Japanese anime, operated by Banzai Anime Club of Alberta (BAKA).
this event has been supplying the participants with various anime regardless of their famousness. and thanks to that this event has expanded to the current scale.
Animethon is organized by the non-profit organization, Alberta Society for Asian Popular Arts (ASAPA).
Firstly Animethon was a one-day event only to screen Japanese anime in two classrooms at Grant MacEwan school.
The whole university was used as a venue from the second event. Doujinshi booths, educational panels and merchandising section also appeared at that time.
The number of participants has increased and distributors also begun to focus on different visitors with different ages and genders.
