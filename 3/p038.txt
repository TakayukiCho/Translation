Summary:
The mission of Animazement is introducing Japanese culture and language through anime and manga. 
The event gathers people for 3 days to enjoy Japanese entertainments and visual culture due fans, volunteers, staff, guests and panelists from Japan and the USA.
The event is planned and held with the festival atmosphere to be comfortable for families, covering a wide range of genres such as anime, manga, J-POP from games, martial arts, traditional crafts and other. 
This event is not only a party, it is also the place for participants to learn something new and to deepen their understanding of Japanese culture.
