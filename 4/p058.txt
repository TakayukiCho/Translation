Summary:
AEx is the festival of anime, manga, video games and entertainment, which is held twice a year and organized by Arcadia Libre Productions.
It boasts a very high popularity in Chile, and was hosted as the event by fans for fans.
AEx gathers various activities such as live performances, cosplay, karaoke, illustration contest, workshops and fans interchange section, video game room, shop zone, artist zone, Japanese culture zone.
Besides, single singers and dance groups, Japanese and worldwide artists related to otaku-culture participate in the event.
