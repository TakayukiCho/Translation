Summary:
Anime Friends is the mega convention specialized in asian pop & fantastic culture such as anime and video games.
The parent event (Anime Friends) is held in Brazil and has been produced and operated by Yamato Corporation since 2002.
The feature of Anime Friends is not only introducing of famous Japanese artists, but also introducing various multi-themes and contests.
The mega convention has been held in Argentina since 2009 and has grown into the biggest cultural event in Argentina's history.
Recent event had over 25,000 participants.
