Summary:
TNT Guadalajara is the Expo TNT's regional event, which is held in Guadalajara, the second largest city in Mexico.
Expo TNT is the biggest event in Mexico, that gathers together fans who loves Japanese anime and manga.
The main feature of the event is performances of Japanese manga, anime, music talents.
The event is held twice a year, in summer and winter.
This event is also a regional competition, the winners will be participate in the World Cosplay Summit.
The main contents of the event are follow:
- Performances of anime voice actors, who dubbed Spanish
- Cosplay competition and costume convention
- Dance competition for idol dance-groups
- Video game tournament
- Art works exhibition
- Merchandise corner
- Live concerts of singers who sing Japanese theme songs / game theme songs
