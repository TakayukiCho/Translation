Summary:
Anime Shuriken is the event, that is held for 1 or 2 days.
Event's schedule is decided depending on the situation of the event preparations and the preparations of the event staff.
The event usually takes place on July's or August's holidays.
It is the oldest event in Cuba for people who like Japanese culture, anime and manga.
The Anime Shuriken's program consists of video projection, sales of action figure, doujinshi exhibition, cosplay, screening of animated music video, video games, karaoke and so on.
Besides, there are different events related to Japanese traditional culture and history: calligraphy, trying on kimono and yukata, samurai and shinobi weapon exhibition, tea ceremony, bonsai trees and others.
Anime Shuriken invites Japan Embassies and Japanese people who are living in Cuba to come and enjoy the event.
