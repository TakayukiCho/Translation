Summary: 
Comic Frontier (commonly known as COMIFURO) is a doujinshi sale market that held twice a year in Jakarta, Indonesia, the event was influenced by Japanese Comic Market.
Comic Frontier aims to provide a place for artists and creators interested in local animation, manga, games (and other) to deliver their work to society and fans.
There are both original and parody productions, mainly illustrations, music, games.
