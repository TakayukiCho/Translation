Summary:
SMASH! was started in 2007 and has now become the main convention in Australia.
SMASH!is held for two days under the management of SMASH Inc, a non-profit organization.
It was founded by pop-culture fans for pop-culture fans, to promote Japanese pop-culture industry to all participants.
SMASH Inc will continue to try providing open and casual "place" for art, creation and their community to develop.
