Summary:
Comiday Chengdu Doujin Festival (abbreviated Comiday) - is the doujinshi-sale event hosted by Comment Chengdu Doujin Festival Preparation Meeting (NEKOPURIN WORKS).
It is the largest and most influential doujinshi exchange platform in China, which receives high reputation from many participants.
Participants are not only the clubs from , but there are also a lot of participants from Hong Kong, Taiwan, Japan, approximately 300 exhibitors.
In addition to doujinshi illustrations, manga, anime, games, novels, music and etc, there are another interesting contents such as garage kit figures, pill dolls (puppet show plays mainly in Taiwan, also popular among young people)
Over 30,000 fans from China and overseas participate in the event every year.
Since its inception, Comiday has put "creation" as its core and prioritize original works and interactions among artists.
Clubs, general participants and industry companies rate this event as "the most doujin event, pure doujin event" due to the small number of commercial elements.
