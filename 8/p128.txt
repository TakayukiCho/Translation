Summary: 
Overload is the comics and manga convention that is held every year in New Zeland, it is the event where local manga-artists can represent and sell their works.
It is very exciting, the event includes contests and work exhibitions, anime goods and manga selling.
Organized by the nonprofit organization Overload Committee, Overload focuses on the amateur art community and provides fun environment and an opportunity for everyone to improve their own arts.
