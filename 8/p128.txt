Summary:
Overload is the comics and manga convention which is held every year in New Zealand.
It is the event where local manga-artists can display and sell their works.
the event also includes contests, work exhibitions and sales of anime merchandises and manga.
Organized by the nonprofit organization Overload Committee, Overload focuses on the amateur art community.
It provides a fun environment and an opportunity for everyone to improve their own arts.
