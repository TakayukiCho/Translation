Summary: 
Doujin Market, also known as Doujima, is the largest illustration market of doujinshi in the Singapore.
We deal with both original content and secondary creation work by artists and clubs from Singapore and its surrounding areas.
Not only general participants gather at the event, there are also talented artists from various fields of work such as pop-culture and original handicrafts, digital and traditional arts, multimedia, music, visual novels and etc.
We invite famous industry artists to the event every year and organize workshops, where art-love and talent of young people could bloom.
This event also functions as a market to promote ideas and talents and provide opportunities to match enthusiastic creators and companies.
