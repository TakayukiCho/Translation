Summary:
Funan Anime Festival is a one-week event that is held at the Funan Digitalife Mall.

There are a lot of workshops presented by the invited guests such as cosplayers and photographers, including make-up and prop production, stage lighting, photo retouching and etc.
Besides, there are screenings of latest popular anime and performances by invited artists.
