Summary:
Cosplay Mania is the event focused on cosplay, usually held in autumn or winter in the Philippines.
Various cosplay projects are held during the event and this is why the event becomes enjoyable for everyone: from new cosplayers to veteran cosplayers, from people who enjoyed light versions of cosplay to cosplay-otaku.
Some of the main projects are the followings.
Cosplay Contest, Cosplay Modeling Contest, Cosplay Photo Contest, Cosplay Video Contest, Cosplay Workshop, Cosplay Tournament.
Cosplay Mania has been held since 2006 by Cosplay.ph, a website for Philippines' cosplayers.
