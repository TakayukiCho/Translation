Summary:
The biggest anime event in Jordan.
The scale of the event grew every year and there was recorded 400 participants at the 8th event on July 2015.
The event was held as fan-meeting before the 9th event in 2016 year, since then the event was changed into the format of convention, and there were about 1,100 visitors in this year.
In addition to the cosplay competition, there are various booths: manga corner, where participants can freely show their illustration skills, booth for studying about Japanese language and Japanese culture, and  enjoy Japanese traditional culture such as origami.
Each participant can bring his own personal computer and talk with others about their favorite anime works while watching them together at the special exchange corner,  there is also goods selling sections (T - shirts, key chains).
