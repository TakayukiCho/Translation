Summary:
CharaExpo is the event operated by Bushi-road Singapore, which was held for the first time in 2015.
This event provides the chance for fans of Japanese content (anime/manga/game/card game/cosplay) to interact with creators during the event.
In addition to the exhibition booths and the sales booths, artists who rarely participate in overseas events comes to CharaExpo.
Besides, the event provide an opportunity for all visitors to interact with guests on the special "Entertainment Stage".
There were exhibitions not only for adult or teenagers, who are basically the anime and manga fans, but also exhibitions for children (infants and elementary school students) such as Doraemon or Crayon Shin-chan.
