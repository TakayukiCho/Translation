Summary:
The international event "Asia Breeze", focusing on the modern visual culture, was organized in 2004 year by Hakuryu team. 
This event is very important for young people living in the center of Russia such as Ural and Siberia.
The contents of the event are following:
- promotion of modern visual culture and culture value of the world, 
- promotion of healthy lifestyle, 
- promotion of interests and activities for young people by various hobbies.
Asia Breeze is an annually event handling cultures and entertainment, mainly focus on visual culture.
The main target of this event is young people.
40% of the participants come from different cities and 2% are come from different countries: Belarus, France, Ukraine, Czech Republic.
Participation age limit is 12 years old or more.
