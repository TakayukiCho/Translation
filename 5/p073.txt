Summary:
AniCon is the event that every year gather Japanese culture's fans from all Eastern Europe.
Guests can enjoy 3 days program and meet favorite characters from anime and manga, cartoon and movies, books and games!
There is a rich show stage perfomance: burning dancecovers, cosplay and fashion show (participants are not only adults, but children too), karaoke perfomance: songs of various stylies,  original anime screenings and arcade game exhibitions.
The friendly atmosphere of the event is another special opportunity to get acquainted with all participants and spectators!
