Summary

Otakon is an asian pop-culture event (anime, manga, music, movies, video games and etc.) that gathers fans of pop-culture and takes place every summer.
The event is held from Friday to Sunday. 
A lot of small events are held with the theme of pop-culture during the festival.
Otakon is operated by the nonprofit organization Otakorp Inc.
