Summary:
Anime Friends is not only a festival of Japanese culture in Latin America, it is also a major event for all anime, manga and game fans.
It records a total of more than 120,000 participants every year, not only from Brazil and other Latin American countries, but also from Japan, it is the big event that consume the average of 150 US dollars per person in merchandising and food sales.
